Accessibility Statement

Grace Connect’s commitment to inclusive access for adult church members and administrators.

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

1. Commitment

Grace Connect aims to provide a respectful, usable, and accessible experience for adult church members, pastors, administrators, and ministry teams, including users with disabilities.

2. Accessibility Goals

Grace Connect should aim for clear typography, sufficient contrast, readable labels, meaningful icons, accessible form fields, keyboard/screen-reader-friendly pages where possible, descriptive alt text, and plain-language legal documents.

3. Ongoing Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing process. New features, mobile operating system changes, and design changes should be reviewed for accessibility impact.

4. Feedback

Users may report accessibility barriers to support@graceconnect.app. Please include the device, operating system, app version, page or feature, and a description of the issue.
