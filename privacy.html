Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

1. Information Collected

When you register a church or join as a member, we collect necessary personal information including your name, email address, phone number, and church affiliation details.

2. Church & Member Data

Any data uploaded to your church's digital space (such as prayer requests, attendance, giving records, and community feed posts) is strictly associated with your church and managed by your Church Administrators.

3. How Information is Used

We use your information to provide the Grace Connect platform functionality, authenticate users securely, facilitate church communication, and send important service updates.

4. Email Communication

We will send automated emails regarding account verification, church approval statuses, password resets, and critical platform updates. We do not sell your email address to third parties.

5. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Authentication and data are managed securely via industry-standard protocols.

6. Supabase/Backend Storage Notice

Grace Connect utilizes Supabase for secure cloud database storage and authentication. Your data is encrypted at rest and strictly governed by Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure that only authorized members of your church can access your church's data.

7. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time. Church Administrators can also request the complete deletion of their church's instance from the platform.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact our privacy team at privacy@graceconnect.app.
